## Why Great Documentation Matters

High-quality documentation is essential for the success of any product or API. It empowers your users to:

- Get started quickly and efficiently
- Find answers to their questions without support
- Understand best practices and advanced features
- Integrate your product into their workflows

Investing in documentation leads to happier users, fewer support requests, and increased adoption.
